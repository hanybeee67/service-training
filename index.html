Service Quiz

Test what you've learned from the training manual.

Q 01 · GUEST GREETING
What is the maximum time to acknowledge a guest after they enter?
✅ Within 30 seconds — the Everest "30-Second Rule." Even if busy, make eye contact and acknowledge the guest immediately.
Q 02 · UNIFORM
Which is correct according to Everest uniform policy?
✅ Long hair must be tied back. Perfume should be minimal. Gel nails are prohibited (food contamination risk). Personal clothing over uniform is not allowed.
Q 03 · TABLE SETTING
How are cutlery, glass, and wet tissue set up at Everest?
✅ All placed on the brass plate — fork, spoon (no knife), glass, and wet tissue are all set on the brass tali plate together. Napkins are pre-set on the table separately.
Q 04 · COMPLAINT HANDLING
What does "E" stand for in the HEART complaint model?
✅ Empathize — genuinely understanding and acknowledging the guest's feelings is the second step of the HEART model.
Q 05 · FOOD SERVICE
From which side should food be served to a guest?
✅ From the guest's right side — food is served from the right. Beverage refills are served from the left. This is international service standard.
Q 06 · FOOD SAFETY
What is the food safety "Danger Zone" temperature range?
✅ 5°C ~ 60°C — bacteria multiply fastest in this range. Keep cold food below 5°C and hot food above 60°C when serving.
Q 07 · MENU KNOWLEDGE
Which drink is BEST for reducing spiciness?
✅ Mango Lassi — the casein protein in dairy yogurt binds with capsaicin and effectively neutralizes the burning sensation.
Q 08 · BRANCH KNOWLEDGE
Which branches use glass tumblers instead of bronze cups?
✅ Suwon, Hanam, and Magok use glass tumblers. All other branches use traditional bronze cups.
